Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the scanning feature?
Point your device camera at any text you want to read. The app will automatically detect and process the text. Make sure you have good lighting for best results.
Why does the app need camera access?
Read It Kid uses your camera to scan and recognize text. Without camera access, the app won't be able to perform its main function.
Is my child's data safe?
Yes! Read It Kid is designed with child safety and privacy as top priorities. We don't store any scanned content, and all text processing is done with privacy protection measures in place.
How do I manage my subscription?
You can manage your subscription through your Apple ID settings. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions on your iOS device.

Troubleshooting

The app crashes when launched
Please make sure you're running the latest version of iOS. If the issue persists, try restarting your device or reinstalling the app.
Text recognition isn't working well
Make sure you have good lighting and the text is clearly visible. Hold your device steady and ensure the text is within the scanning area.
